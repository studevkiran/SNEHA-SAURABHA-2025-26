Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: November 5, 2025

Important: Please read this policy carefully before completing your registration. By registering and making payment, you agree to this refund and cancellation policy.

1. Cancellation by Registrant

1.1 Cancellation Timeline & Refund Percentage

Cancellation Period Refund Percentage Processing Fee
More than 60 days before event 90% refund 10% deduction for processing
45-60 days before event 75% refund 25% deduction
30-44 days before event 50% refund 50% deduction
15-29 days before event 25% refund 75% deduction
Less than 15 days before event No refund Full amount forfeited

Event Start Date: January 30, 2026

1.2 How to Request Cancellation

To cancel your registration, you must:
1. Send an email to: snehasaurabha2026@rotary3181.org
2. Subject line: "Registration Cancellation - [Your Registration ID]"
3. Include: Full name, registration ID, mobile number, and reason for cancellation
4. Cancellation is effective from the date the email is received (not sent)

2. Refund Processing

2.1 Refund Timeline

- Approved refunds will be processed within 14-21 working days from the date of cancellation approval.
- Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used during registration.
- Bank processing may take an additional 5-7 working days.

2.2 Payment Gateway Charges

Payment gateway charges (approximately 2-3% of transaction amount) are non-refundable and will be deducted from the refund amount.

2.3 Refund Calculation Example

Example:
Registration Fee: ₹7,500
Cancellation: 50 days before event (90% refund applicable)
Refund calculation:
- Base refund: ₹7,500 × 90% = ₹6,750
- Less payment gateway charges (2.5%): ₹6,750 - ₹187.50 = ₹6,562.50
- Final refund amount: ₹6,562

3. Transfer of Registration

3.1 Name Transfer

If you cannot attend, you may transfer your registration to another person:
- Transfer requests must be submitted at least 15 days before the event
- Transfer fee: ₹500 per registration
- Both parties must provide written consent
- New registrant must meet the eligibility criteria for the registration category

3.2 Transfer Process

1. Email: snehasaurabha2026@rotary3181.org
2. Subject: "Registration Transfer Request - [Registration ID]"
3. Include: Original registrant details, new registrant details, consent from both parties
4. Pay transfer fee of ₹500
5. Receive updated confirmation within 3-5 working days

4. Special Circumstances

4.1 Medical Emergencies

In case of medical emergencies preventing attendance:
- Submit medical certificate from registered medical practitioner
- Request must be made within 7 days of the event
- Refund may be considered on case-by-case basis
- Maximum refund: 50% of registration fee (subject to approval)

4.2 Force Majeure

No refunds for circumstances beyond control (natural disasters affecting individual travel, personal emergencies, etc.)

5. Event Cancellation by Organizers

5.1 Full Event Cancellation

If the event is cancelled by the organizers:
- 100% refund of registration fee will be processed
- Refunds will be processed within 30 days
- Payment gateway charges will be refunded

5.2 Event Postponement

If the event is postponed:
- Registration will automatically transfer to new dates
- If you cannot attend new dates, standard cancellation policy applies
- No additional fees for date change

5.3 Venue or Schedule Changes

Minor changes to venue location or schedule do not qualify for refunds unless the change is deemed significant by organizers.

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable under all circumstances:
- Processing and administrative fees
- Payment gateway charges
- Early bird discounts availed
- Promotional code discounts
- Add-on services or merchandise (if any)
- No-show without prior cancellation

7. Sponsor Category Cancellations

7.1 Special Terms for Sponsors

For Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Patron sponsors:
- Cancellations more than 90 days: 80% refund
- Cancellations 60-90 days: 60% refund
- Cancellations 30-59 days: 40% refund
- Cancellations less than 30 days: No refund
- Sponsorship benefits (branding, etc.) are non-refundable once executed

8. Failed or Duplicate Payments

8.1 Payment Failures

- If payment fails but amount is debited, it will be auto-refunded by bank within 5-7 working days
- Contact us immediately if not refunded within 10 working days
- Provide transaction ID and payment screenshot

8.2 Duplicate Payments

- Duplicate payments will be fully refunded within 14 working days
- Notify us immediately at snehasaurabha2026@rotary3181.org
- Include both transaction IDs

Phone: +91 98765 43210

10. Important Notes

✓ All cancellation requests must be in writing (email)
✓ Phone requests for cancellation will not be accepted
✓ Cancellation is effective from email receipt date, not event date
✓ Refunds will only be made to the original payment method
✓ Third-party refunds are not permitted
✓ This policy is subject to change; latest version will apply

11. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes regarding refunds will be resolved through mutual discussion with event organizers. The decision of Rotary District 3181 organizing committee shall be final and binding.
